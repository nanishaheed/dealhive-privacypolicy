ProFix AI Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

ProFix AI is a labour booking platform that connects customers with verified skilled professionals. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account & Identity Data

  • Name, phone number, and email address
  • Profile photo for identification
  • Government ID or skill certificates (Labourers only)

1.2 Booking & Service Data

  • Service category and job description
  • Booking history, pricing, and status
  • Payment confirmation (processed by third-party gateways)

1.3 Location Data

Location data is collected only with user consent and only during active bookings to enable nearby matching and real-time tracking.

1.4 AI Chatbot Data

  • User messages submitted to the AI chatbot
  • Anonymous usage analytics to improve responses

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To connect customers with nearby labourers
  • To verify labourer identity and skills
  • To improve platform safety and performance
  • To provide AI-powered assistance

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

  • Shared between users after booking confirmation
  • Shared with map services for navigation
  • Shared with payment providers for transactions
  • Disclosed when required by law

4. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as required for service delivery, legal compliance, and dispute resolution.

5. Data Security

  • Encrypted storage and data transmission
  • Restricted access to sensitive records
  • Secure cloud infrastructure

6. Your Rights

  • Request account deletion
